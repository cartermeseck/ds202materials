Background

Brady Brelinski, Founding Member of the Manhattan Chocolate Society, obviously likes chocolate. He has assembled a database of ratings for plain dark chocolate bars: Flavors of chocolate

By now, over 1900 different chocolate bars have been rated.

Meaning of ratings

Flavors of Cacao Rating System:

  • 5= Elite (Transcending beyond the ordinary limits)
  • 4= Premium (Superior flavor development, character and style)
  • 3= Satisfactory (3.0) to praiseworthy (3.75) (well made with special qualities)
  • 2= Disappointing (Passable but contains at least one significant flaw)
  • 1= Unpleasant (mostly unpalatable)
